Rules, Regulations, Policies, and Hints

Competition

THE ACTIVITY OF DECODING OBSCURED CLUES IN ONE WEBPAGE AND MOVING ON TO THE NEXT (THE COMPETITION OR COMPETITION) IS OPEN TO ANYONE INTERESTED. This competition asks players to find clues that are obscured by any means in the published or other webpages to find the last step or the PRIZE.

Participation of this activity means solving the quizzes or visiting these given pages. If you participate it is assumed that you have read and understood the terms stated here and agreed with them.

The prize on offer is an entry ticket to the Google I/O extended Sri Lanka 2018, livestream and tech party

Data Policy

Your behavior inside this site and it's pages will be tracked, analyzed and be used for ticket verification and any other purpose seen fit by GDG Sri Lanka.

Your session information will be tracked, stored and processed using Cookies and any other method necessary.

Information you provide; email address, name and any other properties will be stored in our database and be used to lay out clues as well as any other purpose seen fit by GDG Sri Lanka.

Rules and Regulations

In order to qualify

  • You must be 16 years or older on 1st January 2018
  • You need to solve ALL the steps, in their SET ORDER. If you find the clues of future steps by other channels, and proceed, we will detect it and disqualify you.
  • Finding and solving the clue is up to the participant. There will be no help given and participants should not expect that help will be given.
  • Participants MUST login at all steps and make sure the login process completes at every step. If the login process is aborted or failed, do not proceed to the next step even if you find the clue. Make sure the login completes. Failing to do so will risk you from being disqualified.
  • Use the same Google account to login to all steps and make sure this account is the one that you will use to redeem the prize
  • Publishing the steps or clues online or offline will immediately disqualify the user and the site may go down.
  • Any publication made with regard to this competition or a part of it gives GDG Sri Lanka the right to share the publication or a part of it on their media channels including the internet with respective credits given to the original author
  • GDG Sri Lanka will not be responsible for bugs inside this code displaying wrong clues for participants, rendering his or her movement inside the website impossible or difficult

Prize handover

GDG Sri Lanka will be the final decision maker on whether you get the prize or not. GDG Sri Lanka holds the right to refrain from awarding the prize to any one or many or even all participants at any time. GDG Sri Lanka has the right to nullify the competition and cancel all tickets given.

Gifts are NON-TRANSFERRABLE. Nobody else may proceed in your place. We will be checking the ID, phone number and email address at the entrance. You may have to login to a given webpage at the entrance to verify the ownership of your email.
